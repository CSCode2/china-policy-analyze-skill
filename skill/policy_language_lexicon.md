# Policy Language Lexicon

## Overview

This lexicon provides plain-language interpretations of common Chinese policy phrases. Policy language is a highly structuredformal register with specific conventions. Mechanical interpretation (treating every word at face value) leads to systematic errors. Use this lexicon as a starting point, always considering document level, issuing body, context, and implementation tools.

## Domestic Policy Language Lexicon

### A

| 政策用语 | 字面解释 | 实际含义 | 强度级别 | 常见含义 | 注意事项 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 按照规定 | According to regulations | Requiring compliance with existing rules; no new flexibility | 中 | 强调现有规则的约束力 | 不是新要求,是重申已有规定 |
| 案件查办 | Case investigation and handling | Active enforcement; investigation to resolution | 中高 | 执法链条完整,不仅是发现还包括处理 | 出现时说明执法有具体案件支撑 |

### B

| 政策用语 | 字面解释 | 实际含义 | 强度级别 | 常见含义 | 注意事项 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 保障 | Guarantee/ensure | Provide institutional or resource support to ensure something happens | 中 | 需要配套措施,不是自然发生 | 通常伴随具体保障措施列举 |
| 必要时 | When necessary | Conditional; not always required, but authority reserved | 低中 | 留有自主裁量空间 | 不意味着"不会",而是"视情况" |
| 必须 | Must | Mandatory requirement | 高 | 强制性要求,违反有后果 | 仅在法律和行政法规中出现效力最高 |
| 变相 | In disguise | Targeting circumvention behaviors, not just direct violations | 中高 | 执法范围延伸至变通做法 | 说明监管部门已关注规避行为 |
| 标本兼治 | Treat both symptoms and root causes | Comprehensive approach: address both immediate problem and underlying cause | 中 | 制度建设与即时处置并重 | 通常意味着会有长期制度建设,不仅仅是专项行动 |
| 并行 | In parallel | Simultaneous implementation of multiple approaches | 中 | 多路径同时推进 | 不同路径之间可能有优先级差异 |

### C

| 政策用语 | 字面解释 | 实际含义 | 强度级别 | 常见含义 | 注意事项 |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 持续推进 | Continue to advance | Continuity of existing policy direction; steady progress, not acceleration | 中低 | 方向不变,节奏不变,不是加速信号 | 不意味着加码,而是延续既有力度 |
| 持续深化 | Continue to deepen | Existing direction with increased depth or scope | 中 | 比持续推进更深一层 | 深化可能涉及更多领域或更严格要求 |
| 从严 | Strictly | Stricter enforcement than before | 中高 | 比当前更严,但不一定是最高强度 | 需要比较"从严"前的标准来判断真实强度 |
| 促进 | Promote | Active support but without mandatory requirements | 中 | 比"支持"更主动,但仍是倡导性 | 是否有实质促进取决于配套措施 |
| 催生 | Catalyze/give birth to | Policy aims to create new formats, models, or industries | 中 | 期待新事物产生的意向 | 催生效果取决于市场响应和技术条件 |

### D

|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 大力发展 | Vigorously develop | Strong support with resources likely to follow | 中高 | 方向明确支持,预期有实质资源投入 | 仍需等待实施文件确认资源投入规模 |

| 做好 | Do well | Competent execution | 中 | 常规性要求 | 强度较低,属于基础执行要求 |

## Usage Rules

1. Always check the lexicon before interpreting a policy phrase.
2. If a phrase is not in the lexicon, interpret it conservatively and note the absence.
3. Context matters: the same phrase may carry different weight in different documents, levels, and sections.
4. Combine lexicon entries with the document analysis framework (policy_reasoning_framework.md) for full interpretation.
5. When a phrase's lexicon interpretation conflicts with the document context, prioritize context and document level.
6. Foreign policy language should be interpreted with particular caution — diplomatic language is deliberately ambiguous and serves communicative functions beyond literal meaning.
7. Never cite a lexicon entry as evidence; it is an interpretive aid, not a source.
